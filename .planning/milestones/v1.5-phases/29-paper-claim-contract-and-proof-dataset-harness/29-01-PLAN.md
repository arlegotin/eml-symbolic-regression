<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| local code -> proof contract JSON | Claim and threshold metadata may be consumed by later CLI/report artifacts as trusted provenance. |
| dataset generator -> artifact manifest | Generated split signatures become reproducibility evidence without storing raw arrays. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-29-01-01 | Tampering | `proof.py` threshold policies | mitigate | Validate known policy IDs and keep `bounded_100_percent` allowed classes limited to verifier-owned training evidence. |
| T-29-01-02 | Repudiation | `proof_dataset_manifest` | mitigate | Include seed, domains, counts, sampling policy, formula provenance, split signatures, and manifest digest. |
| T-29-01-03 | Information Disclosure | dataset manifests | accept | Manifest intentionally omits raw arrays and contains only reproducible synthetic metadata; no secrets or PII are present. |
</threat_model>
