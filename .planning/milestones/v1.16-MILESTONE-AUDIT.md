## Follow-Up Direction

The next milestone should stop spending budget on larger matched i*pi campaigns until the snap/candidate bottleneck improves. The highest-leverage next step is a snap-first exact-candidate workflow: inspect low-margin slot choices, generate bounded exact-tree neighborhoods around failed/loss-only candidates, rank candidates by verifier status before loss, and run a tiny natural-bias recovery sandbox before reopening broad campaigns.
