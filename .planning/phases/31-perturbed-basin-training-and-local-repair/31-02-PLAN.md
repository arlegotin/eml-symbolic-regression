<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| raw perturbed candidate -> repair engine | Failed snapped ASTs and target embeddings are used to propose local moves. |
| repair engine -> benchmark artifact | Repair result can affect bounded threshold evidence. |
| benchmark artifact -> aggregate | Evidence class and repair status drive claim pass/fail reporting. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-31-02-01 | Tampering | `repair_perturbed_candidate` | mitigate | Serialize every attempted and accepted move with source, before/after choices, and verifier status; tests reject unrecorded repair success. |
| T-31-02-02 | Repudiation | benchmark repair payload | mitigate | Preserve `raw_status`, `return_kind`, and raw `perturbed_true_tree.status` after repair so the original failure remains auditable. |
| T-31-02-03 | Elevation of Privilege | `evidence_class_for_payload` | mitigate | Repaired payloads can only become `repaired_candidate`; they cannot receive raw `perturbed_true_tree_recovered`. |
| T-31-02-04 | Denial of Service | repair enumeration | mitigate | Bound target-neighborhood repair by `max_target_reverts=8`; catalog alternatives are disabled and explicit. |
| T-31-02-05 | Spoofing | verifier-gated repair status | mitigate | Accepted repair requires `verify_candidate(...).status == 'recovered'`; status-only or loss-only repair success is rejected. |
</threat_model>
